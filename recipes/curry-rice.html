Curry Rice

Curry rice is an easy dish, perfect for serving with curry. A couple of pantry staples and you'll be on your way to making a fine dish that everyone will enjoy.

This dish is a favorite among Japanese people. It gained popularity as it was easy to prepare for large groups, as well as pre-made mixes being sold in stores all over the globe.

Ingredients

  • Jasmine Rice
  • Onion
  • Garlic + Ginger
  • Turmeric
  • Curry powder
  • Water / Stock
  • Frozen peas (optional)
  • Salt
